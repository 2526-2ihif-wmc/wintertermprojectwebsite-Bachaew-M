Minecraft in general

The Game Minecraft is a sandbox/survival game, chosen by the player what kind of a world they would like to play.
You can take blocks from your surroundings and craft or build something out of them, explore your surroundings,
take on monsters in search of rarer blocks when in a cave.
See the mysteries that the world has to offer, despite you being the one who made it and
play it in your own style, slow or fast as it does have an ending.
Make buildings that you are proud of, survive close calls, build farms as you need food too,
these farms can be automatic by building it so, that it can be efficient.
